Motivation and Ideology

We are driven by a commitment to reshaping the way resources are sourced, products are made, and communities are supported. Our goal is to create sustainable, high-quality goods that reflect Canadian culture and values, challenging traditional models of production and consumption.

Resource Development

We work tirelessly to find truly Canadian resources, overcoming the challenges posed by global outsourcing. Partnering with universities and local groups, we aim to discover, develop, and utilize local materials for a sustainable, ethical product line.

Creation and Innovation

Through collaboration with creators, we develop innovative products that highlight Canadian craftsmanship and creativity. Our focus is on providing the tools, resources, and support to help creators build products that are both sustainable and meaningful.

Community Engagement

We believe in the power of collaboration and are actively working with local businesses, schools, and community groups to foster long-term, sustainable growth. By building strong partnerships, we support the development of local industries and create a more resilient economy.

Market and Sales

Our marketplace serves as a platform to sell not only our own products but also those created by other local artisans and businesses. By offering sustainable, high-quality products, we aim to make ethical consumption the easy and logical choice for consumers.

The Vision

Our vision is to build a self-sustaining ecosystem where Canadian resources are prioritized, and businesses are empowered to thrive. We are working towards creating resource hubs and a CRM system that will streamline the development of local products and services, creating a lasting impact on communities and industries.

S.T.A.R.T

We guide creators through the entire process, from ethically sourcing Canadian resources to delivering meaningful products. Our goal is to empower creators with practical skills and cultural understanding, fostering business growth and community-driven innovation. We envision a future where every product tells a story and creators grasp the full journey from resource to creation.

Our Approach

We guide the entire creative process, from ethically sourcing Canadian resources to delivering high-quality products. Our focus is on both the practical skills and the cultural significance that shape everything we create.

Student Mentorship

Through Riipen, we provide college and university students with hands-on project experience to help them gain valuable skills for their future careers. This mentorship ensures students can apply their education to real-world business challenges.

Affiliate Tools

We offer affiliate tools that support business growth, enabling entrepreneurs to access essential resources for success. These tools provide the foundation for businesses to scale effectively while focusing on sustainability and creativity.

Workshops and Education

Currently in development our courses will be designed to teach both practical skills and the cultural and historical context behind every product. By engaging with these sessions, creators will gain the knowledge and purpose needed to craft meaningful work that resonates with its history and impact.

Long-Term Vision

We aim to build a sustainable ecosystem where creators understand the full scope of their process, from the resources they use to the products they create. This vision fosters community growth and supports a cycle of innovation and cultural connection.

Resource Mapping

We are currently identifying Canada’s abundant but underutilized natural resources with the goal of bringing them into sustainable, mainstream production. Our research focuses on finding new ways to ethically access and refine these resources to benefit various industries.

Data Insights

We’re collaborating with universities to create an interactive map that identifies the location of natural resources across North America. This tool will help pinpoint key resources and highlight areas where manufacturing facilities for processing these materials are already established.

Manufacturing Hubs

We’re partnering with universities to identify the best regions for building manufacturing hubs that support local resource processing. These locations are selected based on factors like infrastructure, resource availability, and access to skilled labor.

Innovation Partnerships

By partnering with top universities and colleges, we gain valuable insights into the latest technologies and methodologies for refining raw materials. These collaborations help us advance sustainable practices in both resource extraction and manufacturing processes.

Local Economies

Our efforts are focused on creating manufacturing hubs that support regional economies by leveraging local resources and talent. These hubs will serve as centers of innovation, fostering growth in both industry and community while promoting sustainable development.

Creation Foundation

The foundation of every product begins with sustainable sourcing from The Reservoir or other local producer, where natural resources are carefully chosen for ethical use. This process ensures that each item starts with materials that respect both the land and its people, creating the groundwork for a responsible creation cycle.

Collaborative Process

Where creators, artisans, and community partners come together to bring raw materials into usable forms. It’s a collaborative, hands-on process that connects people with the materials they work with and brings the story of the land into each product.

Impactful Storytelling

Every product created tells a story that reflects not only the vision of its maker but also the culture and heritage of the community. Through Stream Weaver, the process connects the past, present, and future, ensuring that the product tells a meaningful story from beginning to end.

Sustainable Outcome

The final product is a testament to the efforts of every person and resource involved in its creation. By focusing on sustainability, creativity, and ethics, we ensure that our products are not only high-quality but also contribute to a future where people, the environment, and businesses thrive together.
